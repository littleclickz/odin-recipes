Lemon Balm Cookies

Description

You'll love the herbaceous, citrusy zing of these lemon balm cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 cup softened, salted butter
  • 1⁄2 cup confectioner's or powdered sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 4 tbsp chopped fresh lemon balm
  • 2 tsp fresh lemon zest or finely chopped lemon rind

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, beat together the butter, chopped herb, fresh lemon zest, and sugar until well mixed.
  2. Add the flour and mix well. You may need to use your hands, but be sure not to overwork the dough after the mixture has combined.
  3. Once the dough is smooth and coming together, roll it into a cylinder shape. Wrap tightly in parchment paper and chill for about 2 hours.
  4. Once chilled, slice about 1⁄4" thick. Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, about 1" apart.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes on a rack placed in the top third of your oven. If your oven is on the hot side, be sure to check the cookies after 6 or 7 minutes to make sure they are not burning.
